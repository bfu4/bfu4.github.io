Why make this site?

  1. Dinneen Assigns the Work

    ... Kinda. We don't really know what it is, or when it's assigned, or when it's due. But we think that he assigned it.

  2. We Do the Work

    The work we know of, at least. When the WAAG isn't filled in, the assignments are made up on the spot, etc, it can be hard.

  3. It's not Dinneen's Fault

    He's good. It's not his fault. The previous two points kinda imply he's doing bad, but I want to be clear that he's definitely not. Everyone's a bit unorganized this year. We just want to be able to do the work without calling classmates and asking for help.

  4. So this Site has Answers

    Take them! They're free and cool and fresh :D Probably don't use them all the time, because you need to learn the assignments, but if you need them they're here.

  5. Also, it's Not Cheating

    Mr. Dinneen asks that students who understand the material help other students. This is literally the same thing: explaining the answers. Of course, I'll take it down if Mr. Dinneen asks me directly, but as-is, it's one hundred percent allowed.

How to use this?

There are 3 sections: CodeHS, classwork assignments, and homework worksheets. Each one contains useful information about that specific part of class, broken down into whatever organization system Dinneen uses. Feel free to use these answers, filled worksheets, and in-class assignments however you like!
